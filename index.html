What We Do

We strive to make
your life a bit easier

Here at Scribe, we are dedicated to minimizing the headaches associated with getting a document notarized through offering a platform that allows you to connect with a Scribe notary from your remote location. All you need is an ID, computer with a web cam, mic, and online connection to video chat with an eNotary public who helps you get your documents notarized wherever you may be in no longer than a few minutes.

How It Works

1. Access Scribe

Create an account and input all required information for identity verification purposes.

2. Upload docs

Upload your document and connect with an available notary.

3. Connect

Video chat with a live notary as they go through the notarization process with you.

4. Notarized

Get document signed and notarized in a matter of minutes .
